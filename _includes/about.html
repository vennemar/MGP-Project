  • 3500 BC

    The Wheel

    The Wheel was a major invontion. It simplified moving heavy loads, and enabled many of the great feats of humanity. The wheel was such a foundational technology that it is still in common use today.

  • date unknown

    Paper

    Another simple yet revolutionary technology. Paper was the invention that enabled ideas to be shared with many people and preserved over many years. In societies around the world, the quality of paper had a huge impact on the progress of technology and society. Without it, society as we know it would not exist.
